About the Event

The Apex Charity Cup is a student-led tournament designed to combine athletics, philanthropy, and community engagement in one high-energy weekend event.

What It Is

A two-day soccer tournament featuring competitive matches, local vendors, sponsor booths, and a community atmosphere that brings people together around a meaningful cause.

Why It Matters

  • All proceeds benefit St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital
  • Creates a real service opportunity through sports
  • Highlights student leadership and community collaboration
  • Connects local businesses with a strong local audience

Our Impact

This event is built to do more than host games. It is meant to raise awareness, bring people together, and create tangible support for families affected by childhood illness.

Event Information

The tournament is designed to offer competitive play while creating a welcoming, family-friendly experience for the broader community.

Open Bracket

  • Ages 12+
  • 7v7 format
  • 15-minute halves
  • Open-registration teams

Elite Bracket

  • High school and club-level teams
  • 11v11 format
  • Certified referees
  • Competitive environment

Community Experience

  • Food trucks and vendors
  • Sponsor booths and activations
  • On-site recognition
  • Family-friendly event atmosphere

Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsoring the Apex Charity Cup allows businesses to support a respected cause while gaining visibility among athletes, families, and the local community.

Why Get Involved

  • Support St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital
  • Increase brand visibility locally and on campus
  • Connect with students, families, and community members
  • Receive digital and on-site promotional recognition
  • Show commitment to service and community impact

Sponsorship Levels

  • Food Vendor: Free setup, vendors keep profits
  • In-Kind Sponsor: Prizes or gear with promotion included
  • Direct Sponsor: Booth and social media promotion
  • Premium Sponsor: Expanded branding, announcements, and more
